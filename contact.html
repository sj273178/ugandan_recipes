Frequently Asked Questions

Quick responses to the questions we hear most from our readers, partners and fellow food enthusiasts.

Are all recipes authentic Ugandan dishes?
Yes. Our recipes are rooted in authentic Ugandan cooking traditions. Some are adapted slightly for home kitchens, but we always preserve the original flavour profile and cultural story.
Can I submit my own recipes?
Absolutely. We love hearing from home cooks and food storytellers. Reach out through the contact form with your recipe idea, family history and any helpful preparation notes.
Do you offer cooking classes or workshops?
We periodically host intimate demonstrations, cultural dinners and brand collaborations. Contact us if you would like to book a private experience or partner on an event.
How can I collaborate with TasteUganda?
We welcome collaborations in food media, tourism, hospitality and brand storytelling. Choose “Collaboration” in the subject dropdown and tell us more about your idea.
